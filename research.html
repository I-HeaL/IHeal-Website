Research Areas

Bridging engineering and medicine to create intelligent systems for cardiovascular health.

Core Focus

Interactive Wearable Technologies

We develop smart, unobtrusive wearable devices that not only monitor cardiovascular markers but actively interact with the user to support rehabilitation and daily health management. Our focus is on textile-integrated sensors and soft actuation systems.

Experimental

Hybrid Mock Circulatory Loops

Home to Scandinavia's first hybrid mock circulatory loop, we perform advanced testing of cardiac assist devices. Our test-rigs combine physical fluid dynamics with computational models to simulate patient-specific hemodynamic conditions, including specialized setups for women and pediatric patients.

Total Artificial Heart

Pre-clinical evaluation of Total Artificial Heart (TAH) systems. We investigate the hemodynamic impact of TAH implantation and optimize control strategies to minimize complications such as thrombosis and hemolysis.

Computational

Patient-Specific Modeling

Utilizing Lumped Parameter Models (LPM) and data-driven methods to create Digital Twins of the cardiovascular system. These models enable non-invasive diagnosis and the prediction of treatment outcomes for heart failure patients.
