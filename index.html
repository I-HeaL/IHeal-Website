Research Areas

Soft Tissue Biomechanics

Characterizing the mechanical properties of cardiac tissue to inform better device design and surgical interventions.

Minimally Invasive Devices

Engineering novel tools for catheter-based procedures that reduce patient trauma and recovery time.

Computational Modeling (FEM)

Using Finite Element Methods to simulate heart function and predict the efficacy of various treatments.

Patient-Specific Simulation

Creating digital twins of patient hearts to personalize therapy planning and risk assessment.
