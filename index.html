Pioneering Interdisciplinary Research

The I-HeaL (Innovation for Health and Life) Laboratory operates at the intersection of mechanical engineering, sensor technology, and life sciences.

Our mission is to translate complex biological problems into engineering solutions. From biomechanics to personalized medicine, our diverse team of engineers and clinicians work together to improve patient outcomes worldwide.
